Rescinded guidance documents

Maintaining guidance documents that no longer align with their purpose may result in ineffective use of resources. Rescinding such guidance documents enables both OSFI and industry to focus on the right risks in a timely manner. Find out more about the background of this initiative.

Please contact information@osfi-bsif.gc.ca if you require a copy of a rescinded guidance document for reference purposes.
