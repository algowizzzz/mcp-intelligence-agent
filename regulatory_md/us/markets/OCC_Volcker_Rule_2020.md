# Volcker Rule Final Rule (2020)

**Status:** Source PDF unavailable for automated download (OCC link serves HTML redirect).

**Manual download:** <https://www.occ.gov/topics/charters-and-licensing/volcker-rule/volcker-rule-final-rule-2020.pdf>

**Federal Register:** The final rule was published in the Federal Register on September 21, 2020 (85 FR 61490).  
Issued jointly by OCC, Federal Reserve, FDIC, SEC, and CFTC.

**Key provisions:**  
- Revised definition of "covered fund" to exclude certain foreign funds, credit funds, venture capital funds  
- Simplified compliance framework for smaller banks  
- Clarified permitted activities under the proprietary trading prohibition  
- Effective date: January 1, 2021
